Biography

Shengke Xue received the B.S. degree in communication engineering, from the College of Information Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, P. R. China, in 2015; received the Ph.D. degree with the College of Information Science and Electronic Engineering, Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, P. R. China, in 2020. At present, his research interests include pattern recognition, image processing, and machine learning.

He was a student member of the IEEE. He worked as an assistant editor for Editorial Office of FITEE, Zhejiang University Press (2016.7 -- 2019.5); He worked as an EE engineer for Wenzhou Airuide Sci & Tech Co. Ltd. (2019.7 -- 2019.8); At present, he works as an intern for Alibaba Cloud Intelligence, Alibaba Group.
